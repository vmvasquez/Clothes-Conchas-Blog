Summer Street Fest Series

After being trapped in our houses all winter (and "spring" lets be honest) we cannot wait to get out and do summer right. There is always something going on. Movies in the park, street fests, food fests, music festivals, etc. Sometimes I can’t even keep up with all the stuff that goes on every weekend. It’s a lot but we have to make up for lost time. The food dedicated fests are my favorites; Burger Fest, Hot Dog Fest, BBQ Fest the list goes on. Below are some of the standouts from this summer.

Taste of Chicago

BJ's mustard fried catfish is a Taste favorite. Honestly, it’s one of the only reasons I even bother going to the taste. The crust on the fish is fried to that perfect golden brown color and pairs perfectly with the tangy, yet spicy, signature mustard sauce.The catfish is so good that Chicago’s Best has told its viewers that, I guarantee you that you have not had catfish like this before. BJ’s has been serving Soul Food on Chicago’s Southside since 1997.
