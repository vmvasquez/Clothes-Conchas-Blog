Local Eats

No time to wait to sit at the bar at Mott St for their burger? No worries. make your way down to Mini Mott! The Mott Burger break down: 100% Angus double Patty. American cheese, hoisin aioli, sweet potato frizzles(adds great texture), pickled jalapenos, pickles, miso butter and onions. This burger has a lot going on but it's honestly so simple and delicious. Super laid back staff and atmosphere. Great place to go before you hit up the local bars.

Mahalo's Kalua Pork Platter paired with a Big Island Mai Tai. The pork shoulder was juicy and tender with a zesty BBQ sauce. Mac salad was creamy and refreshing. The ginger sesame slaw added a pop of saltiness (not too much) to the plate. Lastly the Hawaiian rolls we're buttered and perfectly toasted. I plan to go back and check out the macadamia pancakes!

Two tender burger patties, melted provolone cheese, topped off with juicy Italian beef and spicy giardiniera (I may or may not have salivated a bit while breaking this down). This burger is perfection and I am not sorry for eating the whole thing.

This burger is located in the Craft Kave, field level near Gate 2. Inside the bar you can experience what it feels like to be on the field itself. Fans can either sit inside or pay an additional fee to sit outside over the right field bullpen. They currently offer 75 different craft beers as well as domestic standards. The Kave has gained so much popularity that the Sox announced the opening of the Craft Kave express for fans with tickets in the 500 level sections this season. If you find yourself headed to game this season I highly recommend stopping by for some burgers and brews.
