About C&C

People have always told me I should start a food blog so here I am! When thinking of what I should name my blog I wanted to pick something that would make me stand out but also tell you who I am. There are a lot of foodies out there in the world so it can be challenging to find something unqiue. I finally came up with Clothes and Conchas. The clothing part would represent my love of fashion and the concha (a Mexican pastry) bit would illustrate my Mexican hertiage. Also, alliteration is just so much more pleasing to the ears. Please join me on this foodie journey where we try different foods, eat at the newest restaurants and check out an occassional OOTD. People of Chicago/Chicagoland, Welcome to Clothes and Conchas.
