Away from Home

From the moment I drove over the state line from New Hampshire into Maine I was counting down until I could get a Lobster roll. I've had them back home but this was going to be the real deal. Spolier Alert, I was not disappointed. The restaurant I found myself at offered a tio of Lobster Roll Sliders and they were amazing. Picture left to right: butter, lime crema and dill. The buns were toasted perfectly and were the ideal size. The lobster itself was so fresh, tender and probably would have been fine on its own. Out of all of the flavors I would have to choose the butter. It was so simple but so perfect.
